Subi: The Business Acquisition Analysis Tool

Subi is your ultimate guide to evaluating business acquisition opportunities. With a few clicks, get insights into whether a business is worth buying.

Product Overview

The Subi Web App is designed to help potential buyers evaluate businesses listed for sale online. Whether you're a seasoned investor or a first-time entrepreneur, Subi simplifies the process of analyzing a business listing by pulling in key metrics, such as:

  • Asking Price
  • Annual Revenue
  • Cash Flow
  • Location
  • Description of Products/Services
  • Broker Information

Subi uses ScrapingBee to pull data from popular business listing sites and processes it to deliver an intuitive dashboard showing the viability of a business.

Key Features

  • Scraping Integration: Fetches data from multiple listing websites with ease.
  • Custom Queries: Use AI-powered queries to refine the data extraction process (available in advanced mode).
  • JS Rendering: Optional JavaScript rendering for pages with dynamically loaded content.
  • Advanced Mode: Available for registered users, providing additional scraping options and data refinement tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Makes Subi Different?

Subi simplifies data extraction from business listing websites and turns it into actionable insights. Unlike other tools, it focuses on key metrics such as price, cash flow, and broker info, making decision-making easier for the buyer.

What is Advanced Mode?

Advanced Mode allows registered users to refine scraping operations. This is ideal for users needing deeper insights or additional information not available in Basic Mode.
